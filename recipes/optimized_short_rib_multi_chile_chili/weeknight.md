# Weeknight Variant — Optimized Short Rib & Multi-Chile Chili

## Overview
- Delivers core multi-chile flavor with pressure-cooked boneless short ribs and canned beans, targeting total time ≤40 minutes while keeping vector `[4,5,3,6,9,7]`.
- Yields: 4 servings.

## Ingredients
- 1.1 kg boneless beef short ribs, 2 cm cubes (ING-001) — 1.1 kg
- 2 tbsp neutral oil (ING-022) — 30 g
- 2 ancho, 2 pasilla, 2 New Mexico chiles (ING-003/004/005) — 6 each total
- 2 cascabel, 1 árbol, 1 pequin (ING-006) — 4 each total
- 1 tbsp cumin seeds (ING-007) — 9 g
- 1 tsp coriander seeds (ING-008) — 2 g
- 1 onion, diced (ING-017) — 150 g
- 4 garlic cloves, minced (ING-018) — 8 g
- 2 tbsp tomato paste (ING-011) — 30 g
- 1 cup crushed tomatoes (ING-023) — 240 g
- 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth (ING-019) — 240 g
- 1 tbsp soy sauce (ING-015) — 15 g
- 1 tsp marmite (ING-016) — 5 g
- 2 anchovy fillets minced (ING-014) — 10 g
- 15 g unsweetened chocolate (ING-012) — 15 g
- 1 tsp finely ground coffee (ING-013) — 3 g
- 1 tbsp cider vinegar (ING-020) — 15 g
- 1 tsp brown sugar (ING-021) — 4 g
- 1 tbsp hot sauce (ING-028) — 15 g
- 1 can (425 g) rinsed kidney beans (ING-002) — 425 g
- 1 tsp Mexican oregano (ING-025) — 2 g
- 1 bay leaf (ING-024) — 1 each
- 1 minced jalapeño (ING-026) — 10 g

## Equipment
- 6 L electric pressure cooker
- High-speed blender

## Procedure
1. TOAST dried chiles and spices on sauté mode 2 minutes, cover with 250 ml boiling water, and blend with half the onions plus tomato paste, soy, marmite, anchovy, chocolate, and coffee. (Use 250 ml hot water)  
2. SEAR beef in oil on sauté mode until browned (6 minutes), then add remaining onions/garlic for 2 minutes. (Use 30 g oil, 150 g onion, 8 g garlic)  
3. STIR in chile paste, crushed tomatoes, broth, brown sugar, oregano, bay, and beans; lock lid and cook high pressure for 18 minutes. (Use 240 g crushed tomatoes, 240 g broth, 425 g beans)  
4. RELEASE pressure naturally 5 minutes then quick-release, switch to sauté, and simmer 5 minutes to thicken.  
5. FINISH with measured cider vinegar and hot sauce, tasting after each 5 ml increment to maintain sour/heat ratios. (Start with 15 g cider vinegar, 15 g hot sauce)  
6. GARNISH bowls with minced jalapeño. (Use 10 g minced jalapeño)

## Time Summary
- Active time: 28 minutes
- Total time: 38 minutes

## Flavor Fidelity
- Maintains umami stack (anchovy, marmite, coffee) and multi-chile blend; pressure cooking simulates long braise while finishing acid dosing mirrors baseline.
