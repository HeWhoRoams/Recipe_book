## Ingredients
- 2.3 kg beef short ribs (ING-001)
- 45 g beef tallow (ING-022 alt)
- Chile blend as baseline (ING-003/004/005/006)
- 12 g cumin seeds, 6 g coriander seeds, 1 g cloves, 1 star anise
- 500 g onions, 5 garlic cloves
- 20 g tomato paste + 120 g roasted red pepper puree
- 240 g crushed tomatoes
- 600 g black soy beans, rinsed
- 700 g chicken broth
- 30 g soy sauce, 15 g coconut aminos (balance sweetness)
- 10 g marmite, 4 anchovy fillets, 30 g dairy-free cacao, 12 g coffee
- 10 g allulose-brown blend
- 15 g cider vinegar
- 15 g vodka/bourbon
- Bay leaves, Mexican oregano, minced jalapeño, hot sauce as baseline
- Electrolyte finishing salt: 6 g kosher salt + 1 g potassium chloride + 0.5 g magnesium citrate

## Procedure
1. TOAST chiles and spices, soak, and blend paste identical to baseline but include roasted red pepper puree for body.  
2. SEAR ribs in tallow, sweat aromatics, and fry paste; deglaze with vodka/bourbon.  
3. STIR in tomato paste, cacao, coffee, anchovy, soy, coconut aminos, allulose blend, oregano, bay.  
4. ADD beef, black soy beans, crushed tomatoes, pepper puree, broth; simmer covered 120 minutes at 93°C with 20-minute stirs.  
5. REDUCE uncovered 25 minutes to reach thick consistency; skim fat as needed.  
6. FINISH with salt plus electrolyte mix, measured vinegar (15 g) and hot sauce (10 g + 2 g increments) ensuring net carb additions recorded.

## Electrolyte Guidance
- Sodium: baseline salt plus hot sauce provide ~1800 mg; add 1 g finishing salt per serving if sweating heavily.
- Potassium: black soy beans and potassium chloride blend deliver 900 mg per bowl.
- Magnesium: magnesium citrate addition supplies 70 mg per serving; offer toasted pepitas for extra 40 mg if needed.
