# Vegetarian Variant — Optimized Short Rib & Multi-Chile Chili

## Overview
- Reimagines the chili with smoked king oyster mushroom burnt ends and extra legumes, holding flavor pillars while removing meat/fish.
- Serves 8.

## Substitution Map
| Original Ingredient | Substitute | Confidence | Rationale |
|---------------------|------------|------------|-----------|
| Short ribs (ING-001) | Smoked king oyster mushroom burnt ends | 0.68 | Replicates bark and chew after triple sear |
| Anchovy fillets (ING-014) | White miso paste | 0.75 | Supplies salinity and umami without fish |
| Chicken broth (ING-019) | Roasted vegetable stock | 0.78 | Matches body with charred allium stock |

## Ingredients
- 1.8 kg king oyster mushrooms, split lengthwise, scored — 1800 g
- 3 tbsp avocado oil — 45 g
- 600 g cooked kidney beans + 300 g pinto beans — 900 g total
- Chile blend identical to baseline — as specified in baseline
- 2 tbsp cumin seeds — 18 g
- 1 tbsp coriander seeds — 8 g
- 2 large onions, 6 garlic cloves — 600 g onions, 24 g garlic
- 2 tbsp tomato paste — 30 g
- 4 cups roasted vegetable stock — 960 g
- 2 tbsp soy sauce — 30 g
- 1 tbsp marmite — 18 g
- 2 tbsp white miso paste — 36 g
- 30 g unsweetened chocolate — 30 g
- 12 g finely ground coffee — 12 g
- 30 g cider vinegar — 30 g
- 12 g brown sugar — 12 g
- 15 g charred apple cider (NA) — 15 g
- 2 bay leaves, 1 tsp Mexican oregano — 2 leaves, 2 g oregano

## Procedure
1. TOAST dried chiles plus cumin, coriander, cloves, and star anise for 3 minutes; soak chiles.  
2. BLEND soaked chiles, half the onions, garlic, tomato paste, soy, marmite, miso, coffee, and chocolate into paste.  
3. SEAR mushroom halves in avocado oil over high heat for 4 minutes per side until charred; dice into 3 cm pieces.  
4. SWEAT remaining onions/garlic, then fry chile paste for 5 minutes until oil separates.  
5. DEGLAZE with charred apple cider, reducing for 90 seconds; stir in tomatoes, brown sugar, oregano, bay.  
6. COMBINE mushroom burnt ends, beans, vegetable stock, and remaining chile liquor; simmer covered at 93°C for 90 minutes with 20-minute stirs.  
7. REDUCE uncovered 20 minutes to concentrate; adjust salt.  
8. FINISH with measured cider vinegar (15 g + 5 g increments) and hot sauce (10 g + 2 g increments); garnish with jalapeños.
