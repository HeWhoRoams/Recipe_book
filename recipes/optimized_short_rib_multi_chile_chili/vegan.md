# Vegan Variant — Optimized Short Rib & Multi-Chile Chili

## Overview
- Builds upon the vegetarian burnt-end mushrooms while ensuring zero animal-derived ingredients or processing aids.

## Substitution Log

| Unsweetened chocolate | Certified dairy-free 85% cacao | 0.90 | Verified vegan processing |
| Optional crema garnish | Whipped aquafaba foam | 0.78 | Provides richness without dairy |

## Ingredients
- 1.8 kg king oyster mushrooms, scored — 1800 g
- 3 tbsp avocado oil — 45 g
- 900 g cooked beans (kidney + pinto) — 900 g
- Chile blend as baseline — as specified in baseline
- 4 cups kombu-mushroom broth — 960 g
- 2 tbsp soy sauce — 30 g
- 1 tbsp marmite — 18 g
- 2 tbsp white miso paste — 36 g
- 30 g dairy-free cacao — 30 g
- 12 g finely ground coffee — 12 g
- 30 g cider vinegar — 30 g

## Procedure
1. TOAST dried chiles and spices for 3 minutes; soak chiles in 90°C water.  
2. BLEND soaked chiles, half the onions, garlic, tomato paste, soy, marmite, miso, cacao, and coffee until smooth.  
3. SEAR mushrooms in avocado oil until edges char; dice to 3 cm.  
4. SWEAT remaining onions/garlic, fry chile paste 5 minutes until oil separates.  
5. DEGLAZE with charred apple cider and reduce for 90 seconds.  
6. FOLD in crushed tomatoes, kombu-mushroom broth, beans, and reserved chile liquor.  
7. SIMMER covered at 93°C for 90 minutes with 20-minute stirs; monitor bean creaminess.  
8. FINISH uncovered 20 minutes, season with salt, incremental vinegar and hot sauce additions, and optional aquafaba foam dollop per bowl.

## Micronutrient Plan
- B12: Add 6 g nutritional yeast per serving stirred in before service.
- Iron: Mushrooms plus legumes provide 18 mg; pair with vitamin C from jalapeños.
- Calcium: Kombu broth and fortified nutritional yeast deliver 25% DV.
