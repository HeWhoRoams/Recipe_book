# Original Recipe: Optimized Short Rib & Multi-Chile Chili

## Concept Brief
- Triple-seared beef short ribs broken into bite-sized cubes.
- Base paste built from ancho, pasilla, New Mexico, cascabel, árbol, and pequin chiles toasted and blended.
- Maillard depth enhanced with espresso-ground coffee, unsweetened chocolate, soy sauce, anchovy, and marmite.
- Acidity from crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, cider vinegar, and finishing apple cider vinegar splash.
- Slow simmer with kidney beans, onion, garlic, cumin, coriander, cloves, star anise, bay leaves, and Mexican oregano.
- Garnish path: minced fresh chiles, hot sauce, cilantro, pickled onions (optional).

## Ingredient Snapshot
- 5 lb bone-in beef short ribs, trimmed
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil plus more as needed
- 2 cups dried kidney beans, soaked overnight
- Chile paste: 4 ancho, 3 pasilla or mulato, 4 New Mexico, 4 cascabel, 2 árbol, 2 pequin
- Spice trio: 2 tbsp cumin seeds, 1 tbsp coriander seeds, 4 cloves, 1 star anise
- Aromatics: 2 large onions, 6 garlic cloves
- Umami stack: 2 tbsp tomato paste, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp marmite, 4 anchovy fillets, 30 g unsweetened chocolate, 1 tbsp finely ground coffee
- Liquids: 3 cups chicken broth, 2 cups crushed tomatoes, 2 tbsp cider vinegar, 1 tbsp vodka or bourbon
- Finishers: 1 tbsp brown sugar, 2 bay leaves, 1 tsp Mexican oregano, minced Thai bird chile, hot sauce to taste

## Method Snapshot
1. Toast dried chiles, soak until pliable, blend with soaking liquid, aromatics, and umami boosters into a smooth paste.
2. Sear short ribs in vegetable oil on all sides (triple sear) to develop fond; reserve.
3. Bloom spices, onions, garlic, and chile paste in rendered fat; deglaze with vodka/bourbon.
4. Combine paste, seared beef, beans, crushed tomatoes, broth, tomato paste, soy sauce, marmite, anchovy, coffee, chocolate, bay, oregano, brown sugar.
5. Simmer covered until ribs shred tender and beans creamy (~2.5 hours), skimming fat.
6. Finish with cider vinegar, fresh chile garnish, and controlled hot sauce additions.
