## Ingredients
- 1.8 kg king oyster mushrooms, scored (ING-001 substitute)
- 3 tbsp avocado oil (ING-022 secondary)
- 600 g cooked kidney beans + 300 g pinto beans (ING-002)
- Chile blend identical to vegetarian recipe (ING-003/004/005/006)
- 2 tbsp cumin seeds (ING-007)
- 1 tbsp coriander seeds (ING-008)
- 1 tsp cloves, 1 star anise (ING-009/010)
- 2 onions, 6 garlic cloves (ING-017/018)
- 2 tbsp tomato paste (ING-011)
- 2 cups crushed tomatoes (ING-023)
- 4 cups kombu-mushroom broth (ING-019 alt)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (ING-015)
- 1 tbsp marmite (ING-016)
- 2 tbsp white miso paste (ING-014 substitute, vegan-certified)
- 30 g dairy-free cacao (ING-012 alt)
- 12 g finely ground coffee (ING-013)
- 30 g cider vinegar (ING-020)
- 12 g brown sugar (ING-021)
- 15 g charred apple cider (ING-027 NA)
- 2 bay leaves, 1 tsp Mexican oregano (ING-024/025)
- 2 minced jalapeños (ING-026)
- 20 g fermented hot sauce (ING-028)
- Kosher salt plus optional aquafaba foam garnish (FORT)

## Procedure
1. TOAST dried chiles and spices for 3 minutes; soak chiles in 90°C water.  
2. BLEND soaked chiles, half the onions, garlic, tomato paste, soy, marmite, miso, cacao, and coffee until smooth.  
3. SEAR mushrooms in avocado oil until edges char; dice to 3 cm.  
4. SWEAT remaining onions/garlic, fry chile paste 5 minutes until oil separates.  
5. DEGLAZE with charred apple cider and reduce for 90 seconds, ensuring alcohol evaporated.  
6. FOLD in crushed tomatoes, brown sugar, oregano, bay, kombu-mushroom broth, and reserved chile liquor with beans.  
7. SIMMER covered at 93°C for 90 minutes with 20-minute stirs; monitor bean creaminess.  
8. FINISH uncovered 20 minutes, season with salt, incremental vinegar and hot sauce additions, and optional aquafaba foam dollop per bowl.
