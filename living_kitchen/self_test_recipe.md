## Dish Concept
- Minimalist lentil stew for framework validation.
- Serves 2; Flavor vector target `[3,4,2,4,5,2]`.

## Ingredient Snapshot
- 120 g dried red lentils (ING-ST-001)
- 400 g vegetable broth (ING-ST-002)
- 80 g diced onion (ING-ST-003)
- 12 g minced garlic (ING-ST-004)
- 10 g tomato paste (ING-ST-005)
- 6 g olive oil (ING-ST-006)
- 3 g smoked paprika (ING-ST-007)
- 2 g cumin powder (ING-ST-008)
- 3 g kosher salt (ING-ST-009)
- 2 g lemon juice (ING-ST-010)

## Method Snapshot
1. HEAT 6 g olive oil (ING-ST-006) and sauté 80 g diced onion (ING-ST-003) plus 12 g minced garlic (ING-ST-004) for 3 minutes.
2. STIR in 10 g tomato paste (ING-ST-005), 3 g smoked paprika (ING-ST-007), and 2 g cumin powder (ING-ST-008) for 1 minute.
3. ADD 120 g dried red lentils (ING-ST-001) and 400 g vegetable broth (ING-ST-002); simmer 12 minutes.
4. FINISH with 3 g kosher salt (ING-ST-009) and 2 g lemon juice (ING-ST-010); serve hot.
