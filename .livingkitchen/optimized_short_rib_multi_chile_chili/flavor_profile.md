## Overview
- Cuisine Focus: Tex-Mex chili with Northern Mexican dried chile backbone
- Occasion: Competition chili or cold-weather centerpiece
- Servings: 8

| Axis  | Score | Anchor Ingredient/Technique |
|-------|-------|-----------------------------|
| Sweet | 4 | Dark brown sugar bloom to round chile heat |
| Sour  | 5 | Fire-roasted tomato plus cider vinegar finish |

## Texture Goals
- Primary Texture: Spoon-coating gravy with shreddable short rib morsels.
- Secondary Texture: Creamy beans suspended with visible chile flecks.

## Aroma Cues
- Aromatic Backbone: Smoldering dried chile perfume layered with allium fat bloom and toasted spice sweetness.

## Cultural Integrity Notes
- Honor Tex-Mex border traditions through dried chile trinity, bean presence, restrained sweetness, and respect for low-and-slow technique.
